Company Story

JOSHBERN ENTERPRISE was founded in 2016, and we have accomplished so much over the years. To create a world where quality and affordable food are accessible has always been our goal.

Our Founder?

Our Founder and CEO Joshua Akuffo Addo was inspired to start this company by his late father who was also the Eastern region Best Farmer in 1983/4. He would always go to the farm with his father even at a very tender age and watched him provide food for his family and the community. He cultivated a natural love for agriculture and has since then been in the industry.

What we encourage?

At JOSHBERN ENTERPRISE we encourage our community to always consume wholesome food; this we do by not compromising on quality in our production and commodity aggregation.
